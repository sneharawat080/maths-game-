LOGIC LEAP GAME - Instructions

Welcome to the Game. Your goal is to answer math propositions and avoid obstacles.

Use the "True" and "False" buttons to answer propositions. If you answer correctly, the player character will jump.

You have three lives represented by ❤️. Each time you collide with an obstacle or answer a proposition incorrectly, you'll lose a life.

Your score will increase with each correct answer. Try to achieve the highest score possible!
